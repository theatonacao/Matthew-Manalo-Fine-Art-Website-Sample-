W E L C O M E     T O    M A T T H E W    M A N A L O    F I N E    A R T

"Art is such a vast concept. As such, it is the expression of human creativity and imagination whatever it may be: love, romance, the pursuit for happiness or purpose. For me, it is the appreciation for things that are simple. Everyday things in life are often overlooked and we fail to realize the abundance of the extraordinaire. As subjects, human faces are my favorite choice because I find it fascinating how there can be so much meaning just from a portrait. Generally, I like making trivial things the subject for my paintings. I've done, water, leather, and even cellophane. Through my art, I emphasize and magnify the ordinary, and let it be known that there is something extraordinary found in the ordinary; that there is indeed beauty in simplicity."

-Matthew Manalo
